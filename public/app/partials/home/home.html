Evezown

Evezown is a unique commerce and community platform for women. If you are an entrepreneur, service professional or an organisation/association, Evezown offers a unique web presence combined with interaction and transaction features and a host of Ecom and digital promotional tools.

EvezPlace Products and Services market place has five categories - Health, Wellness & Fitness, Food & Nutrition, Beauty & Fashion, Education, Care & Parenting, and Lifestyle. It provides you the ideal platform to sell or buy, to build and promote your brand within your community and digitally on the web.

Wopportunity assists you to resource talent for your organisation. You can find opportunities to earn and further your career too. Evezown membership is currently open to all women.
